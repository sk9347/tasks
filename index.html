Haveli is a high-end casual dining restaurant best known for its widespread buffet which serves lip-smacking North Indian, Chinese and sea food delicacies to its guests. The elegant and artistic interiors of the place radiate warmth. The menu of the restaurant promises to take care of the varied choices of its diners. The place is perfect for for every age group to dine in. A well-stocked bar completes the rich experience of Haveli. The restaurant is located in Lifestyle Building, Begumpet, Secunderabad.
